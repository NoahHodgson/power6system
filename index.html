About

The Power 6 System is a TTRPG system tooled for creating immersive, fully customizeable settings.

The goal of this project is to move away from the a dice set and complicated mechanics. All that is needed to play is six d6 dice.

Basic-Rules

Goals of power6

No Hitpoints

Rather than having one health pool, instead players have 6 body parts that matter.

Characters have 2 arms, 2 legs, body, head. Each of these parts have 6 wound types, ranging from mild to severe.

One Dice Type, Simple Rules

To play a power6 game, all you should need is 6 six-sided dice, pen, and a piece of paper.

This is part of the philosophy of power6: the game is only as complicated as the table wants.

No Classes

There are no classes or races or anything of that nature in the basic rules of power6. While they maybe added into some special modules and are free to be included into some homebrew, the system wants goes a different route.

Instead there is a generic skilltree paired with a faction relationship system. These faction relations can give characters new gear and abilities.

Modules

What are modules?

Modules are additional rules that the GM can mix and match to create the perfect custom setting. May range from cyberpunk futures to feuding vampire houses.
