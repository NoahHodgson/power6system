About

Power 6 is a D6 TTRPG system tooled for jumping into customizeable, immersive settings.

The goal of this project is to create a portable playset that can be easily understood by new players and applied to various settings.

Basic Concepts

Goals of Power 6

One Dice Type, Simple Rules

To play a power6 game, all you should need is some six-sided dice, pen, and a piece of paper. Once you learn the base rules, the modules will be easy to pickup and play.

Branching Progression

There are no classes. Instead there are various skill trees paired with a faction relationship system. These faction relations can give characters new gear and abilities.

Modules

What are modules?

Modules are additional rules that the GM can mix and match to create the perfect custom setting. May range from mutant apocolypses, space operas, and traditional fantasy.

Include features like new factions, weapons, abilities, and enemies.
